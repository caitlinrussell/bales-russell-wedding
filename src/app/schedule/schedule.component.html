    The Railyard

    The Railyard is a newly-developed district in downtown Lincoln featuring shops, restaurants, and entertainment. Check out the big screen, go ice skating, or grab a burger and a beer.

    Lincoln Children's Zoo

    You don't need to be a child to enjoy this zoo. Despite its thorough Larry the Cable Guy endorsements, there are lots of great treasures here, from their headliner Amur leopard to a tribe of lemurs to giant tortoises. It is set amidst a very relaxing neighborhood in Lincoln and is next to Sunken Gardens.
